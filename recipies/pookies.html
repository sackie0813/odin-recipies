Pookies

Description

Pookies are cookies and pancake hybrids

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/3 cup medium dark chocolate
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
Directions
  1. Blend all of the indgredients into a blender or food processor until fully combined
  2. Scoop the dough into circles as you would a traditional cookie
  3. Bake for 8 minutes at 350 degrees farenheit
  4. Wait until golden brown at the edges and the center puffy
  5. Enjoy!
